用到了（类似）对角线法则和分段控制的思想。

Since \(\{g_i\} \rightharpoonup g, \{h_i\} \rightharpoonup h\), \(\int fg_ih_i - \int f gh = \int f(g-g_i)h_i + \int fg(h_i - h)\). Choose a subsequence of \(\{g_i\}\) (denote as \(\{g_i\}\) also)such that \(\forall j, |\int f(g-g_i)h_j| \leq 1/2^i\), of course, \(|\int f(g-g_i)h_i| \leq 1/2^i\). Then \(\int f(g-g_i)h_i \to 0\), \(\int fg(h_i - h) \to 0\). Let \(f = J_n^{\epsilon,\delta}(x,y,z)\), we have proven that \(K_{g_i,h_i}^{\epsilon,\delta}\) converges to \(K_{g,h}^{\epsilon,\delta}\) by point. By the estimation in (A), since \(J \leq e^{-\delta x^2}\), \(K_{g_i,h_i}^{\epsilon,\delta}\) is uniformly bounded. Similarly for \(\gamma \in (0, \delta)\), i.e., \(e^{\gamma x^2} K_{g_i,h_i}^{\epsilon,\delta}\) converges to \(e^{\gamma x^2} K_{g,h}^{\epsilon,\delta}\) by point, and \(e^{\gamma x^2} K_{g_i,h_i}^{\epsilon,\delta}\) is uniformly bounded. So \(e^{\gamma x^2} K_{g,h}^{\epsilon,\delta}\) is bounded.

Then \(\forall \epsilon > 0, \exists r_\epsilon > 0\) such that \[ \int_{\mathbb{R}^n - B(r_\epsilon)} |K_{g_i,h_i}^{\epsilon,\delta}|^p dx < \frac{\epsilon^p}{4}, \int_{\mathbb{R}^n - B(r_\epsilon)} |K_{g,h}^{\epsilon,\delta}|^p dx < \frac{\epsilon^p}{4}, \] and in \(B(r_\epsilon)\), by Lebesgue Dominated convergence thm, \(\exists I\), such that when \(i > I\) \[ \int_{B(r_\epsilon)} |K_{g_i,h_i}^{\epsilon,\delta} - K_{g,h}^{\epsilon,\delta}|^p dx < \frac{\epsilon^p}{2}. \] That is, \(||K_{g_i,h_i}^{\epsilon,\delta} - K_{g,h}^{\epsilon,\delta}||_p < \epsilon\).

HLS inequality

对于HLS不等式最佳情形的证明，需要利用的有共性变换\(D\)和重排\(\mathcal{R}\)。在这两种变换下\(\mathcal{R}D\)不等式是不减的，且函数强收敛到同一函数的不同倍数，即找到了一个最优解。而后先验地利用最优求出一般最优解的形式，将最优解代入即可求出最佳常数。所以开始要做一些开发对称的准备工作。并注意到共性变换\(D\)和重排\(\mathcal{R}\)是有一定竞争的。

文中所说的定理可参见【Dudrovin-Fomenko-Novikov】138页定理15.2 Liouville定理，定理15.3。证明稍微有点长，但应不困难。

In the proof of Young inequality, symmetry is very important, so will it do in the proof of HLS inequality.

No matter transition or rotation, the action act on \(f,h\) at the same time. (no, it is not true, can be seperately).

  1. 伸缩需要对\(f,h\)同时作用。反演变换，单点紧化。公式(2)(3)直接计算，最后得反演变换保角。反共形可能是指将增加变成了减少。

  2. 球极投影：\(1+|x|^2 = \frac{2}{1+s_{n+1}}, |x|^2 = \frac{1-s_{n+1}}{1+s_{n+1}}\). \[ |\dot{u}-\dot{v}| = \frac{2}{1+|z|^2} |\dot{x}-\dot{y}| \Rightarrow \dot{u}\dot{v} = \frac{4\dot{x}\dot{y}}{(1+|x|^2)(1+|y|^2)}. \]

  3. 在同一点附近，\(|\dot{u}|/|\dot{v}| \approx |\dot{x}|/|\dot{y}|\)知小三角形边之间比例近似相等。而并非比例完全相等。又保角，故称为保形映射或共形变换。

  4. 有定理表明Euclid群（平移旋转）以及伸缩和反演生成了所有共形变换。还有定理说\(\mathbb{R}^n\)上的共形变换群同构于\((n+1,1)\)维的Lorentz群。

  5. \[ \mathcal{I}\circ S^{-1}(s) = \mathcal{I}\left(\frac{s_i}{1+s_{n+1}}\right) = \left(\frac{x_i}{||x||^2}\right) = \left(\frac{x_i (1+s_{n+1})}{1-s_{n+1}}\right) = \frac{s_i}{1-s_{n+1}}, \] which implies \(S \circ \mathcal{I}\circ S^{-1}(s) = (s_1, \cdots, s_n, -s_{n+1})\).

  6. 注意共形变换作用到函数上时为了保范公式(8)，需要配上Jacobi矩阵公式(7)。公式(12)(13)中指数\(2\)要变成\(n\)。

HLS 不等式的共形不变性

  1. \[ \int_{\mathbb{S}^n} \int_{\mathbb{S}^n} |J_{\mathcal{S}^{-1}}(s)|^{1/p} |J_{\mathcal{S}^{-1}}(t)|^{1/p} |s-t|^{-\lambda} f(\mathcal{S}^{-1}(s)) h(\mathcal{S}^{-1}(t)) ds dt\\ = \int_{\mathbb{R}^n} \int_{\mathbb{R}^n} |J_{\mathcal{S}^{-1}}(s)|^{1/p} |J_{\mathcal{S}^{-1}}(t)|^{1/p} |x-y|^{-\lambda} \left[\frac{4}{(1+|x|^2)(1+|y|^2)} \right]^{-\lambda/2} f(x) h(y) |J_{\mathcal{S}}(x)| |J_{\mathcal{S}}(y)| dx dy\\ = \int_{\mathbb{R}^n} \int_{\mathbb{R}^n} |x-y|^{-\lambda} f(x) h(y) dx dy, \] note that \(p = 2n/(2n-\lambda) \Rightarrow n/p = n - \lambda/2\) in the last step.

  2. 等距变换是指\(\mathbb{S}^{n}\)上的等距变换，包括反演。而之前已证明在平移和伸缩下的不变，从而共形不变。

  3. \(f\)不恒为\(0\)是重要的，否则上确界可能达不到。

竞争对称性

  1. 想要尽量利用对称性，一个是重排，另一个是共形变换。但两者有些抵触，即共形变换后不一定径向对称。举例如下： 和\(\vec{n}\)垂直的平面就是原来的\(\mathbb{R}^n\)。 Let \(\tilde{s} = D^{-1}s\), then \(f(\tilde{s}) \to \vec{n}\), that is \(D^{-1}s = \vec{n} \Rightarrow s = D\vec{n}\). \(c = F(s) = \left(\frac{1+|x|^2}{2}\right)^{n/p} f(s)\) implies that \(f(s) = c \left(\frac{1+|x|^2}{2}\right)^{-n/p}\). \[D^{-1}s = (s_1,\cdots,s_{n+1},s_n) = \bar{s} \Rightarrow \bar{x}_i = \frac{\bar{s}_i}{1+\bar{s}_{n+1}} = \frac{s_i}{1+s_n} = \frac{2x_i}{|x+a|^2}, i\leq n-1,\] \[\bar{x}_n = \frac{-s_{n+1}}{1+s_n} = \frac{|x|^2-1}{|x+a|^2},\] and \(\frac{1+|\bar{x}|^2}{2} = \frac{1}{1+s_n} = \frac{1+|x|^2}{|x+a|^2}\).

  2. Denote \((Df)(x) = (D^\ast f)(x), (DF)(s) = F(D^{-1}s), (\mathcal{R}f)(x) = f^\ast (x)\).

  3. Then we will prove that \[f_k = (\mathcal{R}D)^k f \to h_f = ||f||_p h = ||f||_p \left(\frac{2}{1+|x|^2} \right)^{n/p}.\]

  4. Note that \(\mathcal{R}, D\) all preserve the norm. Only need to prove the theorem in a dense subset of \(L^p(\mathbb{R}^n)\). Since \(\forall \epsilon > 0, \exists K,g\) such that \[ ||f-g||_p < \epsilon/3, ||g^k-h_g||_p < \epsilon/3, \forall k>K. \] Then \[ ||h_f - f^k||_p \leq ||h_f - h_g||_p + ||f^k - g^k||_p +||g^k-h_g||_p < \epsilon, \forall k>K. \] Since \(f\) is bounded and \(h_f > 0\) in a bounded set, then there is a constant \(C\) such that \(f(x) \leq Ch(x)\).

  5. Since \(D\) is equivalent to a coordinate transformation, so \(D\) preserves order.

  6. Helly 选择定理 一致有界且支集均在某个固定区间内，故可取子列在所有有理点都收敛。再利用对称递减，它们在所有无理点也收敛。

  7. \(g\)本身径向对称，\(Dg\)也径向对称，故由4.5节末知\(g = c_0 h = ||f||_p h = h_f\)。并利用序列的递减性 \[ ||h_f - f^{k_l + 1}||_p \leq ||h_f - f^{k_l}||_p. \]

HLS 最佳情形证明

  1. 由单调收敛定理， \[ ||f^m||_p \to ||f||_p, \\ \int_{\mathbb{R}^n} \int_{\mathbb{R}^n} f^m(x)|x-y|^{-\lambda} f^n(y) dxdy \to \int_{\mathbb{R}^n} \int_{\mathbb{R}^n} f^m(x)|x-y|^{-\lambda} f(y) dxdy \int_{\mathbb{R}^n} \int_{\mathbb{R}^n} f(x)|x-y|^{-\lambda} f(y) dxdy. \] So \(\mathcal{H}(f^m) \to \mathcal{H}(f)\). Try to prove \(\mathcal{H}(f^m) \leq C(n,\lambda)\). 下面\(f\)是指\(f^m\)。之所以用\(f^m = \min \{f(x), mh_f(x)\}\)代替\(f(x)\)是为了能有如下控制并进而用控制收敛定理 \[ (f^m)^k \leq C(1+|x|^2)^{-n/p}, \] 类似上节所做。 \[ \mathcal{H}(f^m) \leq \mathcal{H}((f^m)^k) \leq \mathcal{H}(h_{f^m}) = C(n,\lambda), \] 由定义\(\mathcal{H}(h_{g}) = C(n,\lambda), \forall g\)。

  2. \(h_{g}\)形式的函数能够取得等号。下面是要考虑等号取得的一般情况，故不再要求\(f = f^\ast\)，但由重排定理(3.9)知\(f\)至少是一对称递减函数的平移，同理\(Df\)也是。故重排\(\mathcal{R}\)对\(Df\)只起到平移作用，即知\(\mathcal{R}D\)整体是一共形变换。 \[ \lim_{k\to \infty} ||f-C_k^{-1}h_f||_p = \lim_{k\to \infty} ||C_kf-h_f||_p = 0. \] 再由引理4.8知最终\(f\)的形式。

  3. 引理4.8直接计算即可，只需注意到共形变换作用到函数上时要配有相应Jacobi行列式。 \[ \left|\frac{\partial \vec{y}}{\partial \vec{x}}\right| = |x|^{-4n} Det@ \begin{bmatrix} |x|^2 - 2x_1^2 & -2x_1x_2 & \cdots & -2x_1x_n\\ -2x_1x_2 & |x|^2 - 2x_2^2& \cdots & -2x_2x_n\\ \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\ -2x_1x_n & -2x_2x_n & \cdots & |x|^2 - 2x_n^2 \end{bmatrix}\\ = |x|^{-4n} Det@ \begin{bmatrix} 1 & x_1 & x_2 &\cdots & x_n\\ 0 & |x|^2 - 2x_1^2 & -2x_1x_2 & \cdots & -2x_1x_n\\ 0 & -2x_1x_2 & |x|^2 - 2x_2^2& \cdots & -2x_2x_n\\ \vdots &\vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\ 0 & -2x_1x_n & -2x_2x_n & \cdots & |x|^2 - 2x_n^2 \end{bmatrix}\\ = |x|^{-4n} Det@ \begin{bmatrix} 1 & x_1 & x_2 &\cdots & x_n\\ 2x_1 & |x|^2 & & & \\ 2x_2 & & |x|^2 & & \\ \vdots & & & \ddots & \\ 2x_n & & & & |x|^2 \end{bmatrix} = |x|^{-2n}. \]
