Zhen-Peng Xu

Hi! I'm a PhD student at CIM working in the field of quantum information. Most problems I work on deal with theoretical questions in quantum contextuality. Currently, I'm at the University of Seville in the group of Adán Cabello. I'm receiving my PhD under the supervision of Adán Cabello and Jing-Ling Chen.
